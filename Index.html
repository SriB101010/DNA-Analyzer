ATGo

DNA Sequence Analyzer — From Start Codon to Discovery

GC Content Analysis

Measures the percentage of Guanine (G) and Cytosine (C) bases — indicators of DNA stability.

Reverse Complement

Generates the complementary strand read in the opposite direction (5'→3'). Rules: A↔T, C↔G.

Open Reading Frame (ORF) Finder

Finds potential protein-coding regions: starts with ATG, ends with a stop codon (TAA/TAG/TGA), read in codons (groups of 3).

Mutation Detection

Compares a reference (normal) sequence to a test (patient) sequence. Detects substitutions, insertions, deletions, and frameshifts.
